The fabulous parade

The Fabulous Parade is an exhibition representing the marvelous and spectacular vision of contemporary art. It will be an opportunity to rediscover the greatest classics through the costumes of wonderful and legendary characters.
From the idea to the design, through the many disappointments of the costume designers, you will find unpublished interviews with the different directors. But above all, the secrets of the costume makers. They will tell you about their relationship with the costumes and the characters. You may even run into some of our contributors who will tell us about their magnificent collection pieces.
The exhibition will also be an opportunity to discover a variety of remarkable works of art, each telling its own story and captivating the imagination.
This unmissable event will leave you with lasting and dazzling memories. You will find mainly the themes of magic, enchantment, and imagination.
